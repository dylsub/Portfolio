About Me

  Hello. My name is Dylan Subramanian and I am a motivated student programmer and high school student at Neuqua Valley High School. I love everything about basketball and volleyball, but if I am not out there playing, you can find me here working on personal projects. My projects consist of web applications, video games, and computational programming. I am on the computing team at Neuqua, and I have three years of programming experience.
